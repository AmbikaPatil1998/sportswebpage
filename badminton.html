Badminton

Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net. Although it may be played with larger teams, the most common forms of the game are "singles" (with one player per side) and "doubles" (with two players per side).

Rules for playing badminton

  1. The shuttlecock should not be carried on or come to rest on the racquet.
  2. A player should not reach over the net to hit the shuttlecock.
  3. A serve must carry cross court (diagonally) to be valid.
  4. During the serve, a player should not touch any of the lines of the court, until the server strikes the shuttlecock
