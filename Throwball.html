Throwball

Throwball is a sport where there are two teams involved, each standing against the other, divided by the nets in between. Each team is composed of seven players. Players must throw the ball with one hand while jumping without touching or stepping over the attack line.

Rules for playing throwball

  1. The ball is thrown within three seconds after being caught, only from above the shoulder line and only with one hand.
  2. A player can jump when throwing the ball, which can touch the net (but not the antenna).
  3. The player should have contact with the ground when catching the ball
