Carrom

Carrom is played with lightweight wooden discs which make up the Carrom men and Queen plus a heavier disc called a striker. There are nine Carrom men for each team or player plus one red piece called the Queen. Carrom is played by flicking the striker at the Carrom men to propel them into the pockets.

Rules for playing carrom

  1. Players can pocket only their chosen color token. ...
  2. For every carrommen pocketed, the player gets an extra turn.
  3. If a player commits a foul, they either lose their turn or have to return one carrom men as a penalty to the board.
  4. If the player pockets the carrom striker, it is a foul.
